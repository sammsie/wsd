Why is Harry so important?

Harry is an orphaned wizard. The whole premise of the books is good versus evil. Lord Voldemort is Harry's arch nemesis. A spell to end Harry's life backfired and gave Lord Voldemort a half life living as a creature. It is Lord Voldemort's main mission to destroy kill Harry Potter, and destroy all the good in the world. Harry and his friends find themselves on wild adventures year after year. In the last novel Harry and company must find a way to kill Lord Voldemort once and for all.
